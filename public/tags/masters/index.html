Why and How I Got Into Georgia Tech OMSA

May 22, 2022
With the rising popularity of earning a degree online, more education institutions are increasingly offering fully online programs. Naturally, different programs vary in terms of costs, quality, curriculum, and course design etc. In the area of data analytics/data science, Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) offers two programs — the Online Master of Science in Computer Science (OMSCS) and Online Master of Science in Analytics (OMSA). As I am more focused on analytics, I have chosen to enroll myself in OMSA.
